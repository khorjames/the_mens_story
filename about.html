The Men's Story is Singapore Label with Global Reach

International Accolades, and International Collaboration.

Fashion week is a fashion industry event, lasting approximately one week, where fashion designers, brands or "fashion houses" displays their latest collections in runway fashion shows to buyers and the media. The most prominent fashion weeks are held in the fashion capitals of the world, New York, London, Paris, Milan, Singapore and even Kuala Lumpur.

Singapore Fashion Week returns this October 2022 with a strong unprecedented line-up of Asian designers and labels. The event, which is held between 26-30 October, celebrated and honoured the work of Asian designers who have succeeded in the international fashion arena.

At The Men's Story, the opportunity to showcase our latest collections at the runway fashion shows is a recognition of our brand and designs by the fashion industry.

Our Journey

The Men's Story began as a simple tailor shop at Raffles place by offering custom-made suits and shirts for its corporate customers. It soon garnered a reputation of superior craftmanship by crafting garment of superior quality and cut. Today it has grown into a contemporary men's fashion brand with presense in many markets as well as at its home's fashion and international runway.

About Bespoke Shoe

Shoe Artistry was founded in 2012 with the mission to revive a 40 year old handmade leather shoe label and to preserve the dying trade of shoemaking. With the integration of 3D feet scanner , Shoe Artistry is able to create 3D printed customised insole that fits the handmade leather shoes perfectly, providing not just the craft of bespoke made-to-order footwear but also customization with focus on feet wellness.
